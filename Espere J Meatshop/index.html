Our Story

A Family Tradition of Quality

Since 2003, Espere J Meatshop has been a trusted name in Ormoc City's Grand Central Public Market. What started as a small family stall has grown into a beloved establishment, serving generations of loyal customers.

Our commitment is simple: provide the freshest, highest-quality meats at fair prices. Every cut is hand-selected and prepared with the care and attention that only a family-run business can offer.

Janet, the owner, personally oversees every cut and customer, ensuring the same care and quality that has kept families coming back for over 20 years.

Our Homemade Chorizo is our pride, made fresh daily using our own family recipe. A local Ormoc City favorite for over two decades, and now available for delivery within Ormoc and shipping anywhere in Leyte.
